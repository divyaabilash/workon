What is Sumbon

Sumbon is the desire to achieve the highest good – or Summum Bonum in Latin – is within all of us.

We perform acts of giving to make the world a better place for everyone. But this desire to make a difference should be easier to act upon. Taking the friction out of the giving process is one of the key problems SumBon was conceived to solve.

In the age of connected devices, social media and information flow, we believe that there is a more efficient and rewarding way for both donors and charities to engage in a meaningful conversation. Sumbon’s platform makes it easy for donors to identify causes, give thoughtfully, see their impacts and inspire others while providing non-profits with a better way to engage with donors globally.

Registration is easy. Create an account with only your first name, last name and e-mail address and begin browsing non-profits today.
